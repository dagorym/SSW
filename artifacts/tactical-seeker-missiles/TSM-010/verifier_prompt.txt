## Task Summary

TSM-010 integrated active seeker contact detection into normal ship movement
finalization. When a moving ship's turn path passes through a hex containing an
active seeker owned by the opposing player, seeker detonation is now resolved
(via the existing `resolvePendingSeekerDetonationDamage()` seam) before mine
damage is applied. Inactive seekers are explicitly skipped by
`checkForActiveSeekersOnPath`.

## What was implemented (Implementer summary)

The implementer added an end-of-move single facing change to the tactical move phase.
Players may rotate their ship one hex-facing (left or right) at the end of their movement,
consuming 1 MR, provided they have moved the required minimum distance. Key model changes:

- `FTacticalTurnData` gained two new fields: `pendingEndOfMoveFacing` (int, -1=none) and
  `endOfMoveOriginFacing` (int, -1=not set).
- New public API on `FTacticalGame`: `canApplyEndOfMoveTurnLeft()`, `canApplyEndOfMoveTurnRight()`,
  `applyEndOfMoveTurn(int direction)`.
- `finalizeMovementState()` commits `pendingEndOfMoveFacing` to `curHeading`/`finalHeading`.
- `handleMoveHexSelection()` clears pending state (restores heading) when a hex is clicked.
- `FBattleDisplay` gained `m_turnButtonPanel` (wxPanel*), `m_buttonTurnLeft`, `m_buttonTurnRight`;
  the panel is shown in `drawMoveShip()` when in PH_MOVE with a ship selected.
- `FBattleScreen` gained delegation methods `applyEndOfMoveTurn(int)`,
  `canApplyEndOfMoveTurnLeft()`, `canApplyEndOfMoveTurnRight()`.

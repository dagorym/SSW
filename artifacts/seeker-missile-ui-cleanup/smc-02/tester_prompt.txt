## Task Summary

Board clicks during PH_SEEKER_ACTIVATION now activate one inactive seeker at the clicked hex (calling `activateInactiveSeekerAtHex`). The lower status panel lists all currently activated seekers for the moving player as "Deactivate seeker #N (heading H, allowance A)" clickable rows; clicking a row deactivates that seeker (calling `deactivateActiveSeekerByID`). Both board clicks and row clicks trigger `reDraw()` immediately. `drawSeekerMissiles` during the activation phase renders both inactive stacks (so they remain clickable) and active seekers (so activations are visible with heading-based rotation).

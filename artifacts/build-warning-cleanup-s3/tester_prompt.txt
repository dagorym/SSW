## S3 Tester Handoff — Remove unused wxColour locals in tactical

### What was changed and why
Three tactical source files had `wxColour` local variables that were declared but never used, causing `-Wall` unused-variable warnings:
- `src/tactical/FBattleBoard.cpp`: removed unused `red` and `blue` locals in `computeFFRange`, and unused `blue` in `computeBatteryRange`
- `src/tactical/FBattleScreen.cpp`: removed unused `white` local
- `src/tactical/FBattleDisplay.cpp`: removed unused `yellow` local in `drawDefenseList`

No rendering behavior was changed. No dead-code placeholders were introduced.

### Acceptance criteria
- The tactical build no longer emits unused-local-variable warnings from the identified files.
- Tactical rendering behavior is unchanged.
- The source is readable without dead-code placeholders.
